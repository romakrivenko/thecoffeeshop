COFFEESHOP SIBERIË AMSTERDAM

Siberië opened her doors in 1984, almost four decades ago. Siberië is one of the first coffee shops in Amsterdam and is located in one of the most popular neighborhoods of Amsterdam, the Jordaan.

Our staff can offer you the best advice about our different products. We thrive to serve only the best quality of hash, cannabis, and edibles at reasonable prices.
